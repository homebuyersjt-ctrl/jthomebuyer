FAQ

Do I have to accept an offer?
No. Requesting an offer simply starts a conversation. You’re free to review the details and decide what makes sense for you.
How fast can this move?
Our average closing timeline is around 45 days. Depending on the property and your needs, we can sometimes close in as little as 14 days — or structure the sale to allow up to 6 months.

The goal is to give you an offer in hand and peace of mind, even if you’re not ready to move right away.
What kinds of homes do you work with?
We work with virtually every type of residential property — from move-in ready homes, to properties needing minor repairs, all the way to homes requiring a full renovation.

We also regularly make offers on multi-family properties and portfolios of multiple properties.

There are very few situations where we won’t make an offer. If you’re unsure, the best next step is to share the details and let us take a look.
